Location

Our cottage sits in a quiet bay looking over to the Isle of Jura, called Carsaig.

The nearest shop and pub is less than 1km - a short walk or cycle - away, in the village Tayvallich. The village is very friendly and welcomes visitors to use their tennis court, cafe, and join in their events including boat races and ceillidhs.
